Project 4: League of Legends

Jhin bot performance in 2026 LoL esports

Name(s): Aidan Nguyen-Tran · DSC 80, Winter 2026

Research question

Is Jhin more likely to win or lose any given match?

This project uses 2026 League of Legends esports match data (OraclesElixir) to explore Jhin bot pick outcomes, win rates by league, and relationships between gold, damage, K/D, and game result.

Data & methods

We filter for complete games where Jhin was played in the bot position, then perform exploratory data analysis, missingness assessment, hypothesis testing, and prediction (baseline and final model) with fairness analysis.
